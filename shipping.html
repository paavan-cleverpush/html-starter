Shipping information

Nordstrand Retail GmbH ships physical products only — every item in our catalogue is a tangible good packed and handed to a carrier.

Coverage

We deliver to Germany, Austria, Benelux, and France. Other EU countries on request at checkout.

Rates & speed

  • Standard — 3–5 business days; FREE on orders €29+ (otherwise €4.99).
  • Express — 1–2 business days; €9.99 flat.

Tracking

You receive a tracking link by email when the parcel leaves our warehouse. CleverPush may also send shipment status messages — fulfilment remains solely with Nordstrand Retail GmbH.

Large / heavy items

TVs, small appliances, and bulky homeware may ship on a pallet with scheduled delivery — details at checkout.
